Pricing & Packaging Specification (PPS)
1. Purpose
This document defines the commercial packaging structure for SOTS.
The Pricing & Packaging Specification establishes:
* Subscription plans
* Included capabilities
* Plan limits
* Feature entitlements
* Commercial upgrade paths
* Packaging evolution strategy
This document serves as the authoritative source for all customer-facing plans.
2. Packaging Philosophy
SOTS pricing is designed around customer value.
Customers purchase:
* Behavioral visibility
* Workflow understanding
* Quality confidence
Customers do not purchase:
* Events
* Logs
* Traces
* Internal compute
During Phase 1, packaging shall be based primarily on:
* Applications
* Users
* Storage
* Retention

11. Phase 2 Packaging Evolution
Phase 2 introduces:
* Production Monitoring
* Workflow Health
* Journey Intelligence
Additional packaging dimension:
Included Event Pool
Example:
Team Plan
10 Applications
10 Users
5 Million Events Included
Event Overages Apply
The application-based model remains primary.
Event metering becomes secondary.
12. Phase 3 Packaging Evolution
Phase 3 introduces:
* Autonomous Validation
* Test Generation
* Failure Simulation
* Quality Intelligence Assistant
Additional packaging dimension:
AI Consumption
Example:
Business Plan
500 Included AI Credits
Additional Credits Available
AI consumption shall always remain transparent and separately reported.


13. Packaging Governance
Any change to:
* Plan pricing
* Application limits
* User limits
* Storage limits
* Retention limits
* Included features
must be approved through formal pricing review.
Packaging changes must remain consistent with the Pricing Strategy Document.


14. Success Criteria
The Pricing & Packaging Model shall be considered successful when:
1. Customers can select a plan in under five minutes.
2. Plan differences are immediately understandable.
3. Upgrades occur naturally as organizations grow.
4. Pricing remains aligned with customer value.
5. Packaging supports all Phase 1 commercial objectives.
6. Future Phase 2 and Phase 3 monetization can be added without restructuring existing plans.
