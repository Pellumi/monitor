Billing & Subscription Specification (BSS)
1. Purpose
This document defines how subscriptions, billing, invoicing, payments, renewals, upgrades, downgrades, plan enforcement, taxation, and subscription lifecycle management operate within SOTS.
The Billing & Subscription Specification establishes:
* Subscription models
* Billing rules
* Payment processing
* Currency support
* Invoice generation
* Subscription lifecycle management
* Upgrade and downgrade policies
* Overage billing framework
* Enterprise billing procedures
This document is the authoritative source for all commercial billing behavior.
2. Billing Philosophy
SOTS bills customers based on value received.
Phase 1 customers purchase:
* Applications
* User seats
* Storage
* Quality visibility
Customers do not purchase:
* Events
* Logs
* Traces
* Internal platform compute
Billing shall remain simple, transparent, and predictable.
No hidden billing mechanisms are permitted.
To support local adoption in Nigeria, SOTS provides a Nigeria-only Local plan with reduced limits and localized pricing.

Default Trial:
14 Days
Available For:
* Local
* Solo
* Team

Restrictions:
* No multiple concurrent trials
* One trial per organization
11. Upgrade Policy
Upgrades take effect immediately.
Billing is prorated.
Example:
Upgrade:
Local → Solo
Remaining Days:
15
Customer charged:
Difference × Remaining Billing Period
12. Downgrade Policy
Downgrades become effective at the next renewal.
Reason:
Prevent accidental data loss.
Customer receives warnings if new plan limits are exceeded.
13. Renewal Policy
Monthly Plans
Renew every 30 days.
Annual Plans
Renew every 365 days.
Automatic renewal enabled by default.
14. Failed Payment Handling
First Failure:
Payment Retry
Second Failure:
Past Due
Third Failure:
Grace Period
Grace Period Duration:
14 Days
After Grace Period:
Subscription Suspended
15. Suspension Behavior
Suspended organizations:
Allowed:
* View Existing Reports
* Export Existing Data
Blocked:
* New Demonstrations
* New Session Recording
* New Application Creation
* New Report Generation
16. Invoice Generation

* Overage Charges
* Enterprise Contracts
Invoice Contents:
* Organization
* Plan
* Billing Period
* Currency
* Tax
* Total
* Invoice Number

21. Enterprise Billing
Enterprise Customers Support:
* Annual Contracts
* Purchase Orders
* Bank Wire Payments
* Custom Pricing
* Multi-Year Agreements
* Self-Hosted Licensing
Enterprise invoices may be Net 30.
22. Subscription Governance
Billing changes must:
* Preserve transparency
* Preserve customer trust
* Remain aligned with PSD
* Remain aligned with PPS
* Be auditable
* Be documented
No hidden fees are permitted.
No undocumented overages are permitted.
23. Success Criteria
The Billing System shall be considered successful when:
1. Customers understand billing immediately.
2. Upgrades are frictionless.
3. Billing remains predictable.
4. Phase 1 remains application-based.
5. Phase 2 event billing can be introduced without redesign.
6. Phase 3 AI billing can be introduced without redesign.
7. Enterprise billing is supported.
8. USD and NGN customers can subscribe seamlessly.
9. Nigerian startups and individual developers can access an affordable entry-level paid plan through the Local plan.
