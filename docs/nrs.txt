Non-Functional Requirements Specification (NFRS)
1. Purpose
This document defines the non-functional requirements for the Self-Observing Testing System (SOTS).
These requirements establish the quality attributes, operational constraints, service expectations, and performance standards that govern the platform across all phases of development.
The requirements contained herein apply to all system components including SDKs, ingestion services, processing pipelines, analytics engines, dashboards, reporting systems, intelligence engines, and future autonomous validation capabilities.
2. Scope
The requirements defined in this document apply to:
Phase 1 — Behavioral QA Platform
Phase 2 — Production Intelligence Platform
Phase 3 — Autonomous Validation Engine
3. Requirement Classification
The platform shall satisfy requirements in the following quality domains:
* Performance
* Scalability
* Reliability
* Availability
* Security
* Privacy
* Data Integrity
* Usability
* Maintainability
* Extensibility
* Observability
* Compatibility
* Portability
* Disaster Recovery
* Compliance
* Intelligence Quality
4. Performance Requirements
NFR-PERF-001
Frontend event capture shall not visibly degrade application responsiveness.
NFR-PERF-002
Frontend SDK overhead shall not exceed 2% of client-side execution resources under normal operating conditions.
NFR-PERF-003
Backend SDK overhead shall not exceed 3% of request-processing overhead under normal operating conditions.
NFR-PERF-004
Telemetry collection shall execute asynchronously whenever possible.
NFR-PERF-005
Event collection shall not block user interactions.
NFR-PERF-006
The platform shall support near real-time event ingestion.
NFR-PERF-007
Session replay data shall be accessible within 30 seconds of session completion.
NFR-PERF-008
Behavioral graph generation shall complete within five minutes of processing a recorded session.
NFR-PERF-009
Coverage reports shall be generated within five minutes of analysis completion.
NFR-PERF-010
Dashboard pages shall load within three seconds under normal operating conditions.
NFR-PERF-011
Dashboard searches shall return results within two seconds for standard datasets.
NFR-PERF-012
Workflow analysis queries shall complete within ten seconds for supported data volumes.
NFR-PERF-013
Phase 3 behavioral comparison analysis shall complete within fifteen minutes of deployment data availability.
5. Scalability Requirements
NFR-SCALE-001
The platform shall support at least 10 million captured events per day.
NFR-SCALE-002
The platform shall support at least 100,000 concurrent active sessions.
NFR-SCALE-003
The platform shall support horizontal scaling of ingestion services.
NFR-SCALE-004
The platform shall support horizontal scaling of analytics services.
NFR-SCALE-005
The platform shall support onboarding multiple customer organizations.
NFR-SCALE-006
The platform shall support multi-tenant operation.
NFR-SCALE-007
The platform shall support storage growth without service interruption.
NFR-SCALE-008
The platform shall support behavioral graph generation across applications containing thousands of observed states.
NFR-SCALE-009
Phase 2 production monitoring shall support continuous ingestion from multiple applications simultaneously.
NFR-SCALE-010
Phase 3 intelligence systems shall support historical analysis across multiple software releases.
6. Reliability Requirements
NFR-REL-001
The platform shall provide a minimum event delivery success rate of 99.9%.
NFR-REL-002
The platform shall preserve event ordering within a session.
NFR-REL-003
The platform shall prevent duplicate event processing.
NFR-REL-004
The platform shall detect failed ingestion attempts.
NFR-REL-005
The platform shall automatically retry transient delivery failures.
NFR-REL-006
The platform shall preserve session integrity during temporary service disruptions.
NFR-REL-007
Behavioral graph generation shall produce deterministic results when processing identical datasets.
NFR-REL-008
Coverage calculations shall produce consistent results when executed repeatedly against the same dataset.
NFR-REL-009
Generated reports shall remain reproducible from historical data.
NFR-REL-010
The platform shall detect corrupted processing jobs.
7. Availability Requirements
NFR-AVAIL-001
The platform shall achieve 99.9% monthly service availability.
NFR-AVAIL-002

NFR-AVAIL-003
Analytics services shall degrade gracefully during high-load conditions.
NFR-AVAIL-004

NFR-AVAIL-005
No single component failure shall cause total platform outage.

NFR-SEC-009

NFR-SEC-010

20. Acceptance Criteria
The platform shall satisfy this specification when:
* All non-functional requirements are measurable.
* All non-functional requirements are testable.
* All non-functional requirements are verifiable.
* All requirements support one or more product objectives.
* Each platform phase satisfies the applicable quality standards defined in this document.
